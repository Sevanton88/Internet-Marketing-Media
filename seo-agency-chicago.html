Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How competitive is SEO in Chicago?

Chicago is a top-5 US search market with some of the highest CPCs in the Midwest for legal, finance, and healthcare. The Loop and River North commercial density means downtown verticals are particularly competitive.

Do you target Chicago suburbs?

Yes. Naperville, Evanston, Oak Park, Schaumburg, and other suburbs all have distinct local search patterns. We build suburb-specific targeting for clients serving multiple Chicago metro areas.

What are the strongest SEO industries in Chicago?

Legal, finance, healthcare, and B2B services drive the highest search value. Chicago also has strong restaurant, real estate, and home services SEO markets.

How long does Chicago SEO take?

4-6 months for neighborhood and suburb-specific terms. 8-12 months for competitive downtown terms in legal, finance, or healthcare.

Do you work with Chicago franchise or multi-location businesses?

Yes. Multi-location Chicago SEO requires individual GBP listings, location pages, and local citation profiles for each site. We build and manage these systematically.

What does Chicago SEO cost?

Packages start at $799/month. Competitive Chicago verticals like legal or finance typically require the Growth ($1,499) or Authority ($2,499) package.
