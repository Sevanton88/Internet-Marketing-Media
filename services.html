→ Common Questions

Which service should I start with?

Start with a technical audit - always. It tells us what’s holding your rankings back and informs every other decision. Most sites have 8–12 fixable technical issues that silently cap every other SEO effort.

Do I need all six services?

No. Most clients start with technical SEO + on-page, then add link building as the foundations are in place. We build a scope based on your current situation and competitive landscape.

How do you handle reporting?

Every working day you receive a written update covering exactly what was done. End of each month: a full GSC and GA4 report with keyword movement, traffic data, conversions, and the next sprint’s priorities.

What is the difference between SEO and AEO?

Traditional SEO targets the ranked links in Google results. AEO targets the AI-generated summaries that appear above or instead of those links - in Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini. Both matter in 2026.

Can you work with our existing developer?

Yes - we write developer-ready briefs for every technical change. If you don’t have a developer, we can handle implementation directly in most CMS platforms.

Do you lock clients into long contracts?

No. We work on monthly agreements. Results earn retention - we don’t need a 12-month clause to keep clients.
