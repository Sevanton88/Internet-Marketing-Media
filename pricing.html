Pricing questions

Do all packages include the same quality of work?

Yes. The work quality is identical across all packages. What changes is scope - how many pages we cover simultaneously, how many links we build per month, whether content creation and AEO are included. A Starter client gets the same strategic precision as an Authority client, on a smaller surface area.

Why is there no per-keyword pricing?

Keyword-based pricing incentivizes agencies to pick easy wins and ignore harder, more valuable terms. We price by scope of work and time invested - which means our incentives are aligned with yours: rank the terms that actually matter to your business.

Are there any setup fees?

No setup fees. The first month includes the initial audit, which feeds directly into the strategy and execution. You are paying for real work from day one.

Can I upgrade or downgrade between packages?

Yes. You can move between packages at the start of any new month. If your site's situation changes - more competitive market, expanded scope, different priorities - we adjust accordingly.

Do you offer one-time audits without a retainer?

Yes. Standalone technical and content audits are available with a full prioritized action report. These are ideal if you have an in-house team that will handle implementation. Contact us for audit pricing.

What currency are the prices in?

All prices are in US Dollars (USD). Payment is monthly via bank transfer or card.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No long-term contracts. We work on monthly agreements. Most clients stay because results compound - not because they are locked in.
