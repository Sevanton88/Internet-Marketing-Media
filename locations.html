Our global approach

How we work across markets

We are remote-first by design. Every market gets a dedicated strategy - built from local keyword research, not translated from a US playbook.

01

Native keyword research

We research keywords in the language and search engine of your market. US and German SEO use different tools, different volumes, and different user intent patterns.

02

Market-specific strategy

Competition, search behavior, and trust signals differ by country and city. We do not apply a global template - each market gets a strategy built around its specific dynamics.

03

Content in your language

We write and optimize content natively in English, German, Norwegian, Hungarian, Croatian, and Serbian - not translated. Native content ranks better and converts better.

04

Daily updates, monthly reports

Every client in every market receives the same daily update and monthly GSC + GA4 report. Time zone differences do not affect transparency or communication quality.
