Why industry-specific SEO matters

Different trust signals

A law firm needs E-E-A-T signals, bar credentials, and practice area authority. A SaaS company needs topical depth and product schema. The signals that matter are different.

Different keyword patterns

Healthcare searches are informational first, conversion second. Home services are local and transactional. E-commerce is product and category-driven. Generic keyword research misses these patterns.

Different competition levels

Personal injury law is one of the most competitive SEO verticals. Local home services can rank with far less authority. Strategy must reflect reality.
